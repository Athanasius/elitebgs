Elite BGS

A tool to track and manage the Background Simulation (BGS) of Elite Dangerous. Visualise influences and states using graphs and monitor factions and systems. Forget spreadsheets!

Guide Source
For Commanders. By a Commander.
Graphs
Visualise influnce in a dynamic graph that is automatically created and updated.
Monitor
Monitor factions and systems in your dashboard.
Pending and Recovering States
Now you can even check the pending and recovering states of your faction and that too with the current trend.
Edit System Data
Authorised users can even edit and update the data for a system. Perfect when you are 20000 LY away from home.
